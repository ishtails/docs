## Project name

Verity - Proof-of-Value Infrastructure for Consultations

## One-liner

Verity enables outcome-based consultation payments using escrow, autonomous evidence capture, and AI-powered merit settlement.

## Problem

Consultation markets depend on blind trust, creating payment risk for experts and quality risk for learners.

## What we built

- Escrow-first session lifecycle
- Weighted goal pact before sessions
- Autonomous witness recording integration
- AI judging and transparent payout computation
- Claim/dispute settlement screens
- End-to-end demo flow at `/demo/*`

## Architecture snapshot

- Frontend: React + TanStack Router
- API: Hono service layer
- Contracts: session registry + manager
- Integrations: Recall.ai, Chainlink Functions, IPFS

## Innovation

- Converts subjective conversation quality into structured settlement signals
- Aligns incentives for experts and learners via deterministic payout logic
- Improves trust with objective evidence and final settlement path

## Demo story

1. Login and onboarding
2. Host creates and shares a session
3. Learner joins and funds escrow
4. Session runs and enters evaluation
5. Results show payout/refund and claim options

## Impact

- Higher trust in consultation markets
- Lower dispute overhead
- Better performer discovery through merit signals
