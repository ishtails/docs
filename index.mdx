## Welcome to Verity

Verity is a Proof-of-Value protocol that replaces blind trust with **Verifiable Outcomes**. By combining smart escrow, autonomous session capture, and AI-powered settlement, we ensure experts are paid for their impact and learners only pay for what they actually gain.
